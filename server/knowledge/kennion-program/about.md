# About the Kennion Program

The Kennion Program is a health-benefits platform for employer groups,
primarily serving Alabama-based businesses with additional out-of-state
coverage. It combines Reference-Based Pricing (RBP) medical plans with
concierge support, a benefits app, and a Visa card that helps members pay
for care.

## What is Reference-Based Pricing (RBP)?

Traditional insurance pays providers a percentage off their billed charge,
which means costs track whatever the hospital or clinic lists on its
chargemaster. RBP instead pays providers a multiple of the Medicare
reference rate (the rate the federal government has determined is a fair
price for a given service). Members use their benefits the way they
always have — they just get access to all providers who accept RBP
pricing, which is most providers in the country.

RBP keeps premiums meaningfully lower than traditional PPO plans because
the plan does not have to absorb arbitrary chargemaster markups.

## Product structure

Every Kennion medical plan includes:

- **24/7 virtual primary care** (most plans free on the benefits app)
- **Virtual mental health** and **virtual urgent care**
- **Concierge support** for member questions, provider search, and
  claims
- **A benefits Visa card** to help members pay for care at the point of
  service
- **Preventive care** covered at 100% (as required by the ACA)

Plans vary by deductible, out-of-pocket maximum, copays for primary care,
specialists, ER, inpatient / outpatient, and prescription tiers. The full
benefit grid for every plan is loaded into this assistant's context
separately — ask about a specific plan by name.

## Enrollment and effective dates

Group rates are determined by the census the employer uploads (ages,
dependents, work location for rating area) and by the plan chosen.
Employers pick a plan and an effective date; rates are generated
deterministically from the current factor tables and are
"subject to underwriting and final enrollment" until the group is
approved and locked.

## Administration

Claims administration is handled by EBPA or HealthEZ depending on the
product bucket. Members interact with the Kennion benefits app and
concierge team for day-to-day needs; the administrator processes claims
and provider payments behind the scenes.
